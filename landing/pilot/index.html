What the Assessment Measures

The value proposition is simple: take the assessment so we can measure and improve three key areas that usually decide whether a room stays clean—or repeats the same failures.

Area 1

Canopy environment

Where pockets form inside the canopy volume (not on the wall) and what conditions persist long enough to create risk.

Area 2

Airflow + dehu control

Whether circulation and moisture removal are actually shaping the canopy—especially during lights-off and transitions.

Area 3

Triggers + repeatability

Which events repeat (cycles, setpoint changes, irrigation, defol, HVAC modes) and whether fixes hold across comparable runs.
